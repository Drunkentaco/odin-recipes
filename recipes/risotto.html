Sun-dried Tomato & Goat Cheese Risotto

A comforting easy risotto recipe that would impress anyone

Ingredients

  • 60g Baby Arugula (or baby spinach)
  • 1 Lemon
  • 1 Onion (or shallot)
  • 30ml Sun-dried tomato pesto
  • 24g (or 3 1/2 cups) Vegetable broth (or chicken broth)
  • 30g sun-dried tomatoes
  • 165g arborio rice (DON'T USE ANY OTHER RICE)
  • 12g pine nuts
  • 60g goat cheese

Steps

  1. Zest and juice the lemon. Peel, halve and small dice the onion. Thinly slice the sun-dried tomatoes.
  2. In a medium pot, heat a drizzle of olive oil on medium-high. Add onion and cook, stirring frequently, 1 or 2 minutes, until the rice is slightly translucent. Add the broth; season with S&P. Bring to a boil then reduce the heat to a simmer. Cook, stirring occasionally, 20 to 25 minutes, until most of the liquid has been absorbed and the rice is al dente (Still form to the bite).
  3. While the rice cooks, heat a small dry pan on medium-high. Add the pine nuts and toast, stirring frequently (keep an eye on them as they can burn easily). Immediately transfer to a bowl.
  4. Add the goat cheese, 2 tbsp of butter ( double for 4 portions) and Â½ the lemon juice to the cooked rice. Cook for another 2 minutes, stirring constantly; season with S&P to taste.
  5. In a large bowl, combine the arugula, remaining lemon juice and as much of lemon zest as you'd like; drizzle with olive oil and season with S&P to taste, Divide the finished risotto between your plates and drizzle with the pesto. Top the risotto with the toasted pine nuts. Serve the remaining arugula salad on the side. Bon appetit!
