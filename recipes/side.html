Skillet Brussels Sprouts and Potatoes

A perfect winter side dish, be creative, add bacon, sausages, or replace the potatoes with sweet potatoes

Ingredients

  • Potatoes
  • Brussels sprouts
  • Olive oil
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Steps

  1. Wash the potatoes and brussels sprouts.
  2. Cut the potatoes and sprout as shown in picture.
  3. In medium heat, on a heated pan, at the olive oil until shimmering.
  4. Add potatoes. Saut√© for 6-8 minutes.
  5. Add Brussels sprouts and cook for 3-4 minutes. Bon appetit!
